The Evolution of AI in Payments

For over 40 years, the payments industry has been on a journey from rule-based systems to today's sophisticated predictive analytics powered by AI.

1980s–2000s

The Rule-Based Era

Back then, AI was all about following set rules. Systems were rigid, handling tasks like fraud detection and credit approvals mechanically.

2010s

The Rise of Machine Learning

Machine Learning introduced dynamic algorithms that could learn and improve. Fraud detection became smarter and segmentation more accurate.

2020s

Deep Learning and Predictive Analytics

Deep Learning allowed AI to understand unstructured data like images and language. Predictive analytics enabled personalization.

2024 Onward

The Generative AI Revolution

We're entering the Generative AI era. It anticipates fraud, simulates scenarios, and personalizes customer interactions like never before.
